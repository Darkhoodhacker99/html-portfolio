The Best Movies According to Erik

My top 3 movies of all-time:

Pearl Harbour (2001)

A great movie that follows a love story between a soldier and a young lady nurse.

Hacksaw Ridge (2016)

One of my favourite movies. It tells the story of a christian soldier of the WW2 who survived among enemies with no weapon, but served as a frontline medic.

The Notebook (2004)

This movie is a romantic masterpiece. The story of two young lovers that are on different social ranks, will make you cry.
